JOEY TRIBBIAN

Joey comes from an Italian-American family of eight children, of which he is the only son. He is from, and presumably born in, Queens. His grandmother affectionately calls him, "my big fat Joey" telling the gang he weighed twenty-seven pounds at birth. Later Monica's Thanksgiving turkey is about nineteen pounds which Joey tells Rachel, "It's like me when I was born."
